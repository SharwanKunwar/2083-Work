Step 1 — Install from official Microsoft sources

Start with genuine software. Download media only from Microsoft to reduce malware and activation problems.

Windows download portal Microsoft 365 / Office plans

Step 2 — Activate Windows

  1. Open Settings → System → Activation.
  2. Select Change product key if you have a key.
  3. Or sign in with the Microsoft account that holds your digital license.
  4. Wait for status: Active.

Tip

If hardware changed recently, use the Activation troubleshooter on the same screen.

Step 3 — Activate Office or Microsoft 365

  1. Open any Office app (Word, Excel, etc.).
  2. Go to File → Account.
  3. Choose Sign in with your licensed account, or enter your product key.
  4. Confirm the account shows an active subscription/license.

Important

Avoid unofficial "activators" or scripts. They can expose your device to malware and license violations.

Step 4 — Troubleshoot safely

"Windows is not activated" after reinstalling

Run Activation Troubleshooter and sign in with the original licensed account.

Office says "Unlicensed Product"

Check the signed-in account, renew subscription if expired, then restart Office apps.

Product key not accepted

Verify edition match (Home/Pro, Office version) and region/store source.
